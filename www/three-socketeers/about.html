The Ballad of the Three Socketeers

In a realm of bytes and streams so bright,
Three Socketeers rose to fight the night.
Through sockets deep and packets wide,
They forged the paths where data rides.

With clients calling, swift and keen,
And servers waiting, crisp and clean,
They whispered words in silent scroll:
A dance of requests, a perfect poll.

Through ports they leapt, both fast and free,
Bridging gaps in connectivity.
From HTTP’s steadfast grace,
To webs that shift at rapid pace.

One bore Nginx, bold and wise,
A gate where traffic multiplies.
Another wove CGI threads,
Binding scripts where logic spreads.

The third, unseen yet standing tall,
Managed streams, requests, and all.
Their creed remains, their vow is clear:
“All for one, and one for peer!”

So raise a glass, let echoes flow,
To networks vast and data’s glow.
The Socketeers, both brave and free,
Guard the web eternally.
